Kund Mala

Category: Place to chill


About Place:
Kund Mala (कुंडमळा ) is a place close to Begdewadi Railway Station. This place is famous for RANJANKHALGE (रांजणखळगे). These are naturally created potholes in the bedrock of the river. Ranjan Khalge (रांजणखळगे) are natural phenomena when flowing water of river cuts the rock and forms spherical domes inside rocks. The structures are very beautiful and you realize the power of water. It may take thousands of years to form these structures.
Being a lesser known place the crowd will be very less except in monsoon. You will be amazed to see the power of water and the marvels it can create. The environment is pleasant and its a good picnic spot. There are no hotels nearby and there are no proper seating arrangements to enjoy the view. It's kind of an adventurous place too.



Season to visit: Monsoon
Preferred time: Any
Type : Friends, Family
