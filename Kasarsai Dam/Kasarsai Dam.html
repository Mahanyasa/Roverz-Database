Kasarsai Dam

Category: Small Dam


About Place:
Kasarsai dam is a small dam situated near to Hinjewadi IT park, one of the largest IT parks in India. It is located in west Pune. The location is close to residential hubs of the city yet away from the city buzz and rush.The special thing about this place is the peaceful surrounding. It's perfect for a quick trip because of being near to the city. The sun-rise and sun-set is awesome.One can also swim in the dam if he/she knows how to swim.



Season to visit: Anytime
Preferred time: During sunrise , sunset
Type : Family ,Friends
